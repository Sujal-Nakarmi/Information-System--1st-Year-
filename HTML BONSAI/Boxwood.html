Boxwood Bonsai

Bonsai, which translates to "tree in a pot," originated from China and Japan. The Boxwood's tiny leathery leaves, dense growth habits, and rough bark make it a good bonsai tree subject. Since the tree is resilient to different lighting conditions, the boxwood bonsai tree is perfect for both indoor and outdoor environments.


Bonsai Specification


- Age Range: 3-5 Years Old
- Height: 10" - 12” Tall Potted
- Pot: 8” Ceramic Bonsai Pot
- Classification: Broadleaf Evergree
- Level: Beginner
